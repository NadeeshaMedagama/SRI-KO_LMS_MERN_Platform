╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  📊 HOW IT CALCULATES                                             ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                    ║
║  Growth Percentages:                                              ║
║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────       ║
║  Formula: ((current - previous) / previous) × 100                 ║
║  Example: (15 - 10) / 10 × 100 = +50%                            ║
║                                                                    ║
║  Daily Active Users:                                              ║
║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────       ║
║  Counts: Users who logged in TODAY                                ║
║  Compares: Today vs Yesterday                                     ║
║                                                                    ║
║  Course Completions:                                              ║
║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────       ║
║  Counts: Completed courses THIS MONTH                             ║
║  Compares: This month vs Last month                               ║
║                                                                    ║
║  Average Rating:                                                  ║
║  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────       ║
║  Calculates: Average of all course ratings                        ║
║  Shows: Absolute change (not percentage)                          ║
